CHANGES IMPLEMENTED:
====================================================================

1. TRANSPORTATION PROBLEM - Simplified Input Format
   ------------------------------------------------
   
   OLD FORMAT (REMOVED):
   - Required "transportation" keyword on first line
   - Required explicit dimensions "m n" on second line
   - Separate lines for supply and demand
   
   NEW FORMAT (AUTO-DETECT):
   - No keywords needed
   - Auto-detects dimensions from matrix structure
   - Format: Each row = [costs...] supply
             Last row = [demands...]
   
   EXAMPLE:
   19 30 50 10 7
   70 30 40 60 9
   40 8 70 20 32
   7 9 18 14
   
   This is automatically recognized as:
   - 3 sources (3 rows with supply at end)
   - 4 destinations (4 demand values)
   - Balanced: Total Supply (48) = Total Demand (48)

2. HUNGARIAN METHOD - Space-Separated Values
   ------------------------------------------
   
   OLD FORMAT (REMOVED):
   - Comma-separated values: 9,11,14,11,7
   
   NEW FORMAT (SIMPLIFIED):
   - Space-separated values: 9 11 14 11 7
   - Easier to type in exams
   - More readable
   
   EXAMPLE:
   min
   9 11 14 11 7
   6 15 13 13 10
   12 13 6 8 8
   11 9 10 12 9
   10 12 9 11 13
